Meet Our Founder & President

Meet Our Founder & President The exceptional Philanthropist Dr. Dikwa is one of the leading experts in the Public Financial Management (PFM) Reforms in the Nigerian Public Service. He has Doctorate Degree in Accounting and Finance, Master Degree in Finance and Bachelor of Science Degree in Accountancy. He had attended the Senior Executive Course 34, 2012 at the National Institute for Policy and Strategic Studies, (NIPSS) Kuru. He was awarded Certificates in Public Financial Management Reforms, Leadership & Governance Programme, High Performance Leadership Programme and Treasury Management at the Harvard University, USA, University of Cambridge, Oxford University UK and Thames Valley University UK respectively. He was also awarded Certificate in Advance Corporate Finance from the Business School for the World (INSEAD- FONTAINEBLEAU, FRANCE). Dr. Dikwa is a chartered Accountant and member of many Professional Bodies. Dr. Dikwa is a Fellow, Certified National Accountant, Associate Institute of Chartered Accountant of Nigeria, Fellow Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria, among others. Dr. Dikwa has facilitated and attended many Courses, and authored and edited series of Publications to his Credit. Dr. Dikwa has been a season examiner for the Annual Federal Civil Service Promotion Evaluation Examinations for years. Amongst his notable achievements in the Federal Service include direct involvement in the Conceptualization and Implementation of some PFM Reforms such as Government Integrated Financial Management Information System (GIFMIS), Integrated Personnel and Payroll Information System (IPPIS), Treasury Single Account (TSA), International Public Sector Accounting Standards (IPSAS), Presidential Initiative on Continuous Audit (PICA), Asset Tracing Team (ATT), Whistleblowing Policy, Fiscal Sustainability Plan (FSP) and Strategic Revenue Growth Initiative (SRGI). Dr. Dikwa has served as the Borno State Accountant-General and permanent secretary finance 1999 – 2004. Dr. Dikwa was the former Chairman Technical Board, Bank of Industries (BOI) and Bank of Agriculture (BOA). He has served on Board of Transcorp Hilton, Defence Industry Corporation, Transmission Company of Nigeria, Distribution Company of Nigeria (DISCO), Royal Swaziland Sugar Company etc. Dr. Dikwa was the Director in-charge of Ministry of Finance Incorporated (MOFI) later Revenue and Investment Department and Director of Funds both under the Office of the Accountant General of the Federation. Dr. Dikwa acted on various occasions as the Accountant-General of the Federation including the last Acting - Appointment by the President, Muhammadu Buhari, GCFR, on the 12th June, 2015. Dr. Dikwa was the Director, Special Projects and Secretary, Presidential Initiative on Continuous Audit (PICA) under the Office of the Honourable Minister of Finance, until his appointment as Permanent Secretary in the Federal Civil Service. Dr. Dikwa has traversed many countries across the globe and is happily married with children and gr and children.

BRIEF HISTORY

After a decade of protracted conflict that has destroyed economic, social and normal life in Borno State due to the insurgency that took its foot in the North-East region of the country, the current Governor of Borno State, His Excellency Professor Babagana Umara Zulum, mni in 2019 came into the herm of affairs in the State. Part of his transformation agendas was to encourage individual and organizations to invest massively in the State especially in the areas of infrastructure development and education. Al-Ansar Foundation, a non-governmental organization that has been operating in the State decided to respond to this invitation by the State Government through the establishment of the proposed Al-Ansar University Maiduguri, a first private University with the aim of saving foreign exchange by providing alternative quality education in North-East geo-political zone. Having defined a strong foundation for the establishment of the proposed Al-Ansar University, the promoter and influencer of the University; Dr. M. K. Dikwa, mni constituted a Planning and Implementation Committee (PIC) to supervise the processes for its establishment. The PIC was charged with the responsibility of interacting with the National Universities Commission (NUC) on matter relating to the licensing of the proposed University. The PIC was also charged with the responsibility of ensuring that the facilities provided for the take-off of the proposed University are adequate and in line with standard by the NUC. In recognition of the importance of the project to the State, the Executive Governor allocated 100 hectares of land to the Al-Ansar Foundation to build the permanent site of the University. Furthermore, on 4th January 2021, His Excellency, Professor Babagana Umara Zulum performed the grant breaking ceremony of the proposed Al-Ansar University paving way for construction and development of critical infrastructure for the take-off of the University. Consequently, the State government has also granted approval to the Foundation to use “BEWAC” as temporary site to enable the University to commence full academic activities in record time.

OUR VISION

The vision of the proposed AI-Ansar University is “to be a world-class academic institution where faith, innovation and service are nurtured for the advancement of human development".

MISSION

The mission of the proposed AI-Ansar University is 'to be a beacon of knowledge and transformative learning seeking to advance professionalism, build a diverse and socially inclusive society, and be a powerhouse for human capital development. The mission of the proposed University is a demonstration of its goal to produce hard working men and women, who shall endure in their goal to develop and offer service to the Nigerian nation and the world at large.

OBJECTIVES

The law establishing the proposed AI-Ansar University has outlined its objectives aimed at meeting its vision and mission. The objectives of the proposed AI-Ansar University are to:

  • encourage the advancement of learning and to hold out to all persons without distinction of race, creed, sex or political conviction, the opportunity of acquiring a higher and liberal education;
  • provide sources of instruction and other facilities for the pursuit of learning in all its branches, and to make those facilities available on proper terms to such persons as are equipped to benefit from them;
  • encourage and promote scholarship and conduct research in all fields of learning and human endeavour;
  • evolve academic programmes to suit the changing social and economic needs of society through continuous review of curricular and developments of new programmes through programme structural flexibility to respond to societal and technological changes;
  • create and expand access, and opportunities for education, attract and retain quality students, researchers, and teachers, thereby assisting in developing human capital and mitigating the brain drain currently afflicting Nigeria;
  • produce internationally acceptable graduates that could compete favourably with their peers anywhere in the world;
  • carry out basic and applied research leading to the domestication and application of new technology to the Nigerian context through collaborative linkages with other academic and research institutions in Africa and the rest of the world;
  • establish a centre for entrepreneurial studies to stimulate job creation and innovative abilities in students from onset of their studies, in such a way that graduates shall be resourceful self-reliant and job creators; and
  • undertake activities appropriate for teaching, research and community service as expected of a University of high standard.
