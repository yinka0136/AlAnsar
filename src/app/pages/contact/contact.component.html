LOCATION

The proposed Al-Ansar University is occupying a 100 hectares of land located along KM 5, Gubio Road in Maiduguri, Borno State. Borno State is located roughly between latitudes 100 N and 14 No and longitudes 110 300 E and 140 45'. Located in the North-Eastern corner of Nigeria. Borno State occupies the greatest part of the Chad Basin and shares borders with the Republic of Niger to the North, Chad to the North-East and Cameroon to the East. Within the country, its neighbours are Adamawa, to the South, Yobe to the West and Gombe State to the South-West.
